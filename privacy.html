1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DailyTaskBuddy ("the App"), developed by SalvisionX, handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Collection

DailyTaskBuddy does not collect any personal information. We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal details to use the app.

3. Data Storage

All app data — including your tasks, priorities, streak count, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain device information and use cookies or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which you can review at policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Fonts — used to load fonts. Google may log basic request data such as your IP address. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

The App itself does not use cookies. However, Google AdMob may use cookies or similar tracking technologies as part of ad delivery. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

DailyTaskBuddy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
